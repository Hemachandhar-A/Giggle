# 🛡️ GigShield: End-to-End Demo Walkthrough

This document serves as your complete guide to the current GigShield frontend. It outlines every tab, every metric, and the underlying logic (ML & backend) powering the interface. Use this as your script or reference guide while presenting the project to the judges.

---

## 🌐 Global Elements & Header
Available on every screen, these establish the platform as a real-time, production-ready system.
*   **System Status Indicator (Top Right):** Real-time polling to `/api/v1/health` showing database connection status ("All systems operational").
*   **Global KPI Stats Bar (Top):**
    *   **Active Policies:** Total workers currently enrolled and paying premiums across all zones.
    *   **Live Disruptions:** Number of active weather/system triggers currently overriding standard delivery operations.
    *   **Claims This Week:** Total auto-processed claims generated by the pipeline.
    *   **Payouts This Week (INR):** Global total disbursed to workers this week, acting as proof of financial execution.

## 1️⃣ Coverage Tab (The Worker's Portal)
*Highlights individual transparency, ML explainability, and immediate payout ledgers.*

### Policy Details
*   **Worker Context:** Loads details dynamically for the primary worker (e.g., Priya S.).
*   **Weekly Premium (e.g., ₹82.00):** The dynamically calculated cost of insurance.
*   **SHAP Explanations (Premium Factors):** This is where LightGBM machine learning shines. It breaks down the premium calculation in Tamil, showing transparent factors:
    *   *"+₹12 for Rain Forecast in the zone"*
    *   *"+₹8 for High Flood Hazard Zone risk"*
    *   *"-₹5 Discount for 5 weeks of clean (zero claim) records"*
*   **Claim Eligibility:** Verifies if the worker has passed the mandatory 28-day "Age Gate" (anti-fraud measure).

### Zone Risk Profile
*   **Zone Identifier:** Example: *Velachery, Chennai*.
*   **Flood Tier:** High (Tier 3), dictating the baseline risk.
*   **3-Point Weather Sampling:** Mentions our Open-Meteo corroboration engine that samples the centroid and surrounding coordinates to prevent false positives.

### History & Ledger
*   **Claim History:** Shows every claim filed under this policy, along with the **Fraud Score %** and **Routing Decision** (e.g., Auto-Approve, Hold).
*   **Payout Ledger:** Shows actual Razorpay transaction IDs proving cash was sent to the worker's UPI address.

## 2️⃣ Workers Tab (The Enrollment Registry)
*Highlights scaling, partner variety, and dynamic onboarding data.*

*   **Enrolled Workers Table:** Lists all partners in the system with their realistic details (Names generated dynamically from their UPI handles).
*   **Platform & Tier:** Displays which app they work for (Zomato/Swiggy) and their operational zone's flood tier.
*   **Age in System:** Shows "Days Enrolled". You can point out that someone like Priya (30 days) is eligible for payouts, whereas a brand new worker might be rejected until they hit the 28-day mark.

## 3️⃣ Triggers & Payouts (The Live Demo Center)
*Highlights the core execution pipeline. **This is where you fire the demo!***

### Declare Disruption Event (Form)
*   Allows you to simulate a severe weather event (e.g., **Heavy Rain: ≥64.5 mm**, Severe Heatwave, or Platform Suspension).
*   *Business Logic:* Behind the scenes, this creates a `TriggerEvent` using a composite score based on GIS flooding data, meteorology APIs, and active warnings.

### Live Payout Pipeline
*   Once a trigger is fired, you watch the system animate in real-time:
    1.  **Disruption:** Registers the event.
    2.  **Claim Created:** The backend finds all eligible workers in that zone and creates claims.
    3.  **Fraud Score:** Evaluates each claim using the 7-signal ML engine.
    4.  **UPI Payout:** Automatically disburses funds via Razorpay.
*   **Console Log:** A scrolling terminal in the UI displays real-time execution logs (e.g., `"Claim created · Fraud score: 28% → auto_approve"`).

## 4️⃣ Fraud Engine (The Risk Management Brain)
*Highlights the system's ability to protect the insurance pool from bad actors.*

### Model Health Dashboard
*   **Fraud Model Precision (e.g., 92.4%):** Shows the accuracy of the Isolation Forest (IF) + CBLOF ensemble models in weeding out fake claims.
*   **Premium RMSE (₹18.42):** Proves the LightGBM pricing model is highly accurate, predicting the needed premium to within ~₹18 of the actual risk.

### 7-Signal inputs & Routing Rules
*   Shows the exact thresholds for automation:
    *   **< 0.30 (Green):** Auto Approve (Instant UPI)
    *   **0.30 – 0.70 (Orange):** Partial Review (50% paid immediately to alleviate hardship, rest held).
    *   **> 0.70 (Red):** Hold for manual investigation.
*   **Signals Tracked:** Highlights advanced checks like PostGIS Spatial matching, 7-day delivery activity, and Device Fingerprint isolation.

### Fake Weather & GPS Spoofing Detection
*   Explains exactly how the system cross-references claims with Open-Meteo data to prevent workers from claiming "Flood Disruptions" on sunny days, and polygon bounding to detect fake GPS apps.

### Live Claims Analysis
*   After a trigger is fired, this populates with the actual claims assessed. Shows the visual "Fraud Ring" component, highlighting why a claim received a 60% score (e.g., lacked recent delivery activity) versus a 20% score.

## 5️⃣ Admin (The Insurer's Dashboard)
*Highlights the financial sustainability and business metrics of the platform.*

### Loss Ratio by Zone
*   **The Golden Metric:** Calculated as `Total Claims Paid / Total Premiums Collected`.
*   Shows tabular data for each zone. If a zone goes above 100%, it clearly indicates the insurer is losing money in that area, prompting the model to automatically adjust future premiums.

### Enrollment & Retention
*   Tracks Lapse Rate and spots **Adverse Selection**.
*   *Adverse Selection Alert:* If suddenly >40% of enrollments come from High-Tier flood zones (meaning people are trying to cheat the system right before monsoon season), this triggers a warning.

### Model Health & Drift
*   **Premium Drift:** Compares the 4-week premium average against the 12-week baseline. If climate change or unexpected weather causes payouts to spike constantly, the drift alert activates, meaning the ML model needs retraining.
*   **Slab Config:** Verifies if the Swiggy/Zomato payout threshold targets are up to date.
